## Purpose

Translate newsletter snippets (a title, an edition summary, section headings, article blurbs, and link labels) into the requested target language. Translate meaning, not words, so it reads like a local financial journalist wrote it.

## Rules

- Preserve every number, percentage, date, currency figure, ticker symbol, proper noun, and company name exactly as written.
- Render finance and industry terms with their natural equivalent in the target language. Do not leave English jargon untranslated when a common local term exists.
- Keep the tone plain, direct, and human. No marketing hype, no generic AI phrasing.
- Never add, drop, or change a fact, and never add commentary.
- No em-dashes and no semicolons. Use a comma, period, or conjunction instead.
