## Purpose

Take the section drafts and produce the complete newsletter in the exact layout below. Never alter `[Read: ...]()` links or invent content.

## Output format

```
<Title>
<Edition summary>

## Competitive Landscape
<article summary>
[Read: <article title>](<url>)
---
<article summary>
[Read: <article title>](<url>)
---

## Deals & Movements
<article summary>
[Read: <article title>](<url>)
---

## Regulatory & Policy Watch
<article summary>
[Read: <article title>](<url>)
---

## Disruptors & Tech
<article summary>
[Read: <article title>](<url>)
---

## Quick Hits
<article summary>
[Read: <article title>](<url>)
---
```

Keep the five sections in that fixed order. Omit any section that has no articles — do not include an empty section heading.

## Title and summary style

- Title: about 3 to 6 words, a real headline. No company name, no ticker, no leading label or colon prefix.
- Summary: one short sentence (about 20 words) capturing the edition's main thread. Do not open with "This edition", "This week", "In this issue", or "we cover/trace". No em-dashes, no semicolons.
- Tone: plain, direct, human-journalist. No marketing hype, no generic AI phrasing.
